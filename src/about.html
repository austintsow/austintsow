about me

Hello, I am Austin Tsow! I am a current undergraduate student pursuing computer science, based in Sammamish, Washington, but currently in Spokane, Washington. I am a highly motivated, result-oriented individual passionate about building innovative software solutions.

In my previous roles, I have demonstrated my ability to learn new technologies, as well as systems and operations. In my role as a Data Analyst at Century 21, I was responsible for analyzing real estate listings by pulling data from local multiple listing services (MLS) into Excel. I used this data to provide daily updates on real estate activity locally, including new listings, pending sales, and sold properties. As a waiter at Shanghai Garden, I developed a new system to improve restaurant operations which resulted in a 35% increase in overall efficiency.

I have experience in various programming languages such as C++, Java, Python, HTML, and CSS. I am also familiar with debugging, Linux, full-stack web development, and backend software development. My skills have enabled me to build websites, small projects, and solve complex problems using machine learning. I am passionate about using technology to make a positive impact on the world—specifically, areas of education, healthcare, and sustainability.
